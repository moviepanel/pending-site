Movie Panels

Movies & cinema dedicated discussion boards platform.

You want to debate a movie?

You want to make your voice heard? You want to know what others think? Then you need a message board. We'll provide it.

Topic Message boards

Each existing movie or person involved in the production of a movie can have a centralized message boards section, where users can start individual topics related to the movie or the person (just like the "soon to be former" IMDb message boards). Optionally tag and filter topics in a board. If you want to know what people think about the bear scene in Revenant or how Oscar worthy is LaLa Land, you won't have to browse through all the entries. Just simply search on "bear", "Oscar", or related tags.

Making the boards better organized, and more civil

like/dislike option as in a typical social network. Topics with predominant dislikes can be optionally hidden.

Filters for foul language

A possibility to report undetected expressions will be provided to users.
